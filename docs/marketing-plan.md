## Phase 3 — Active Agency Outreach ✅

Find 2–3 more agencies like Matic Digital. The goal of first contact is a specific conversation about a current or upcoming project.

**Priority order:**
1. Warm network first — go back to whoever introduced Matic Digital, scan LinkedIn connections for agency people
2. Build a cold target list of 10–15 agencies (Dribbble, Awwwards, Clutch)
3. Send warm messages first, cold after
4. One follow-up after one week — then move on

**File:**
- `docs/outreach-strategy.md` — target profile, where to find agencies, warm + cold templates, follow-up rule, tracking table

**Status:** Strategy and templates ready. Execution is the next step.
