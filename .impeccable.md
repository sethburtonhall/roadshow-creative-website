## Design Context

### Users
Agencies looking for embedded technical leads + founders needing full-stack direction. Both audiences visit to understand Seth's capabilities and potentially hire him for product work or architecture decisions.

### Brand Personality
Bold, honest, minimal, creative. Confident and technical, but approachable and warm — not intimidating or aloof. Established expert who's accessible.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark editorial meets stage-light boldness. California gold accents (golden hour / production stage feeling) paired with Pacific blue. Grid lines, spotlight glows, large background numerals create intentional composition. Refined minimal with clear hierarchy. Not playful; sophisticated and purposeful.

### Design Principles
1. **Intentional over trendy**: Every design choice serves a purpose; no decorative flourishes
2. **Clarity over cleverness**: Technical audience appreciates directness
3. **Warmth in dark**: Dark theme with gold accents creates approachable confidence
4. **Restrained motion**: Smooth animations serve transitions, not distraction
5. **Editorial composition**: Large background elements and section numerals create design intent, not AI template feel
